Website Design Brief
Design Theme

Modern Minimal • Premium • Motion-Driven • Editorial • Interactive Portfolio

The website should feel clean at first glance but become increasingly immersive as users scroll. Every interaction should have subtle motion, giving the feeling of a high-end creative portfolio rather than a traditional business website.

CSS variables + modern transitions for micro-interactions
Overall User Experience Goal

The website should immediately communicate creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail. It should feel like a premium portfolio where motion enhances the experience without distracting from the work. Every scroll, hover, click, and transition should reinforce a polished, modern identity while keeping navigation intuitive and content easy to explore.
