About

I'm Rishabh Raj, a multidisciplinary designer passionate about creating experiences that combine creativity with functionality. Over the years, I've worked across branding, digital products, motion graphics, web design, and visual storytelling.

My approach focuses on understanding problems before designing solutions. Every project begins with research, strategy, and attention to detail, ensuring the final outcome is not only visually impressive but also meaningful and user-centered.

Whether it's building a brand identity, designing an intuitive interface, producing engaging animations, or developing modern websites, I strive to create work that leaves a lasting impression.
